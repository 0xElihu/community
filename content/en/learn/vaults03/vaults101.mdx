## Why use Maker Vaults?

A vault let’s you put your crypto to work. Instead of selling yoru long term crypto holdings when you need cash, you can borrow Dai against them. Dai is a stable coin, that means you can use it for almost anything you can use dollars for.

TIP: Don’t forget to put your extra Dai to work by earning interest on Oasis Save. With MakerDAO there is always something you can do with your crypto.
